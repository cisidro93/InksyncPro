Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to InkSync Pro. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. Because our application is designed as a local-first utility, your data remains in your control.

2. Data Collection & Processing

InkSync Pro does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers.

All file processing, conversions, and image analyses happen entirely locally directly on your iOS device. We do not have access to your comic files, reading habits, or library metadata.

3. Third-Party Services

While the app operates locally, you may choose to interact with the following third-party services from within the app:

  • ComicVine API: Used strictly when you explicitly request metadata (issue descriptions, volume details) for a comic. Your search queries are sent directly to ComicVine.
  • iCloud: Used if you choose to export files to your iCloud Drive or rely on iCloud device backups. This is governed by Apple's privacy policies.

4. Permissions Used

To provide its functionality, the app requests the following local device permissions:

  • FaceID / TouchID: Used exclusively to unlock the 'Private Vault' feature locally. Biometric data never leaves your device's Secure Enclave.
  • Local Network Access: Required only to establish a direct connection between your phone and your e-reader for Wi-Fi file transfers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@inksyncpro.app.
